and by separately plotting each year's seasonality curve, the conclusion of yearly increasing CO2 concentration is further reinforced. In the chart above, the seasonality curves for 2010-2013 and 2020-2023 are shown. Not only is the year over year increase in the seasonality curves visible, but the incease over a decade. The 2023 curve is showing greater separation from 2022, supporting the NOAA headline (https://www.noaa.gov/news-release/broken-record-atmospheric-carbon-dioxide-levels-jump-again) of the annual increase from May 2022 to May 2023 being the 4th largest since recording began in 1958.
